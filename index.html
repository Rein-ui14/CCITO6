Welcome to the forests of the Philippines, a sanctuary of biodiversity that stretches across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. Each region offers its own unique ecosystem, from the towering dipterocarp forests of Luzon to the lush mangrove swamps of Mindanao, and the rich rainforests of the Visayas. These forests are home to countless species of plants and animals, many of which can only be found in the Philippines. In the Visayas, forests like those in Samar and Negros are vital for protecting endangered species such as the Visayan spotted deer and the rare Philippine eagle. Whether it’s the cloud forests of Mt. Kanlaon or the mangroves lining our coasts, the Visayas region plays a key role in maintaining the country’s ecological balance. Our mission is to safeguard and sustainably manage these irreplaceable forests. Beyond being home to wildlife, they protect our communities from natural disasters, provide livelihoods, and help combat climate change. Join us in preserving these forests for the generations to come.
